Grammar Windsor Hockey Club

Hockey in the heart of Auckland City

Based at Auckland Grammar School's Fuji Xerox sponsored turf on Normanby Rd, Mt Eden and Diocesan school turf, Clyde Street, Epsom. We offer a range of Junior and Youth teams that provides a pathway of development towards our Senior Premier and Division Grades.

Junior & Youth Hockey

Junior hockey comprises mixed, and at higher grades separate, girls and boys AHA competitions over Term 2 and 3. Junior grades cover School Year 1 to 8 (Primary/Intermediate) in team cohorts of Year 1&2, Year 3&4, Year 5&6, Year 7&8.

 

Youth Hockey comprises seperate girls and boys teams and covers Schoole Years 9 to 14 (High School)

Juniors and Youth Subscriptions

Subscriptions are to be paid in full by the final muster in April if not paid at the time of registration.  One single payment is simplest for all, however its timing does allows for staged payments if needed. It also avoids refund administration should your child elect another sport prior to team selection.  Subscriptions can be paid by cash, cheque or internet transfer.  This year we are continuing with our discounted structure.

 

Season Subscriptions remain the same as last year and are as follows (inclusive of Club shorts and shirt uniform):

 

  • Youth (Year 9 - 13) - $275
  • Juniors (School Years 1 & 2) - $185
  • All other Juniors (Years 3 - 8) - $250
  • $10 discount for the 2nd child in the same family
  • $20 discount for the 3rd child in the same family
  • Subscriptions cover the cost of turf hire for practices, AHA fees, upkeep of team equipment, uniforms and goal keeping gear.

 

The clubs bank account details are as follows:

Bank - Bank of New Zealand

Account Number - 02 0160 0253103 000

Account Name - Grammar Windsor Hockey Club Incorporated

 

Please ensure that you include your child’s full name and the team they are playing for (if known) as a reference when making payments by internet transfer or cheque.

 

Enjoy the season!

Club Transfers

 

If you are new to the Grammar Windsor Hockey club this year and either previously played for another Auckland club or for a club elsewhere in New Zealand then a transfer needs to be arranged. Auckland - Club to Club transfers can now be done online so please email us to arrange this for you. If you are transferring from a club elsewhere in New Zealand then a transfer form needs to be completed. Please download the form below and get your previous Club and Association to sign off. Once completed please return to a Grammar club representative so that we can lodge the form with the AHA.
